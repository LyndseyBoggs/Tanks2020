Coward
------------
>Always runs away from the player
>Gains speed as his health decreases *extra*


Swiss
------------
>Patrols his own borders.
>Minds his own business unless you shoot him.
>Will chase you out of his square, then resumes patrol. *extra*
>Resets lastShotBy (goes back to minding own business)


Aggressive (current Inky)
--------------
>Constantly chases you over the map.
>Will flee and rest if health < 0.5health


Sneaky
--------------
>Will chase the player until the player looks at him
>If player looks at him, he runs away
