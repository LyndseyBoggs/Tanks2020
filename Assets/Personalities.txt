

Coward
------------
>Always runs away from the player, does not heal


Swiss
------------
>Patrols his own borders.
>Minds his own business unless you shoot him.
>Will chase you out of his square, then resumes patrol. *extra*
>Resets lastShotBy (goes back to minding own business)


Aggro
--------------
>Constantly chases you over the map.
>Will flee and rest if health < 0.5 health


Blind
--------------
>Stands still until he hears the player
>Will chase and fire at the player while hearing them
> If player escapes hearing, will remain still and rest
